# What does Pieces do?

Pieces harnesses a powerful Long-Term Memory Engine to solve AI's biggest flaw—forgetting past context—by capturing and recalling your entire workflow.

Combined with secure material storage and AI code assistance via your preferred cloud or local LLM, all powered by PiecesOS, you'll never lose track of your work again.
