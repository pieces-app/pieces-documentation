## What does Pieces do?

Pieces is an all-in-one productivity suite built for developers, combining a Long-Term Memory Engine that remembers your entire workflow, a Drive for saving and reusing snippets, notes, and links, and a Copilot that generates code, answers questions, and adds comments using the LLM of your choice. 

Whether you’re juggling front-end layouts, debugging back-end logic, or exploring data science pipelines, Pieces captures your context, organizes your resources, and gives you AI-powered help exactly where you need it. 

Install PiecesOS to power the desktop app and browser/IDE extensions locally—keeping everything secure and private on your machine—and dive straight into preserving your creative flow.

### Pieces Suite

Get started with the Pieces Desktop App or learn more about PiecesOS and Ollama, the two core dependencies required to unlock full Long-Term Memory Engine (LTM-2.5) and local LLM functionality.
